Queues, domains, logic, time travel
- Posts by new users are added to the moderation queue
- Posts by regular users (users with an approved post) are immediately accepted
- Posts by staff users are always added to the moderation queue
- Updated posts by staff users are added to the moderation queue

Monies, domains, aggregation, queues
- Staff users are paid on the number of visits to their posts
- Visits to posts with one author pay 100% to that author
- Visits to posts with multiple authors pay based on when subsequent authors were added and how many visits occurred after the addition
